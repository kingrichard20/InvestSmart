# Observation 1
Most InvestSmart users are unfamiliar with investing and want to spend about 10 minutes per day learning about how to invest.

## Supporting Data

Most users surveyed had low familiarity with investing. On a scale from 1-5, 33.3% of users rated their familiarity as *1*, and 22.2% rated their familiarity as *2*. Only 5.6% of users rated themselves a *4*, and 11.1% rated themselves a *5*. When asked how long they are willing to spend learning to invest per day, most users indicated they would spend 5-10 minutes (22.2%) or 10-20 minutes (38.9%). Finally, the most popular feature users requested was having 5-10 minutes lessons (33.33%), followed by budgeting and savings tools (27.8%), and community discussion and Q&A (22.2%)

## Team Action
- We know that most users desire ~10-minute lessons.
- We know that community discussion will be a popular feature and strongly consider adding it to our app in some form.
